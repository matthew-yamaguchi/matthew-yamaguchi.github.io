## An Introduction ##
Everyone has their own style of writing and speaking.  The words we choose combined with the syntax of our sentences communicate what we wish to express.  The same idea applies to writing code.  Programmers naturally have their own preferences when it comes to specific situations such as deciding how much indentation to add on a new line, naming variables, and determining where to place your opening and closing brackets in functions.
